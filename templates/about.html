How predictions are calculated

Predictions are calculated through the usage of different models using the python library sklearn. Data is gathered from ESPN, USA Today, and crunchtimebaseball.com. Website is built using flask-bootstrap.

Average WAR model:

The Average WAR model correlates a player's average WAR before signing their active contract and the annual salary value of the contract they signed. This model outputs predictions on the low end of expected salaries because a player is generally paid by recent success.

Peak WAR model:

The Peak WAR model relates a player's peak WAR in a single season before signing their newest contract to the annual salary value of their active contract.

Age model:

The Age model relates a player's age at signing their contract to the length of the contract.
